The ENERGENIUS project is a Horizon Europe project designed to transform how citizens, businesses, and communities interact with energy. By leveraging digital tools, AI-driven insights, gamification, and cross-sector innovation, it makes energy management simple, engaging, and beneficial for everyone, striving to achieve democracy in the energy transition.
